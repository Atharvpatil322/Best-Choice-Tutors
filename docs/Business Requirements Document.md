**Business Requirements Document (BRD)**

**Tutoring Marketplace Platform**

**1\. Purpose of the Document**

This BRD outlines the business rationale, objectives, and high-level requirements for developing a **Tutoring Marketplace Platform**. The document focuses on **why the platform is needed**, **what problem it solves**, **who it serves**, and **how it creates value and impact**, without going into technical implementation details.

**2\. Business Background & Context**

The demand for **private tutoring and personalized learning** is rapidly increasing, especially in regions like the UK. However, the current tutoring ecosystem is fragmented, relying on informal referrals, unverified tutors, manual scheduling, and insecure payment processes.

This project aims to build a **centralized, trusted, and scalable digital marketplace** that connects students and parents with qualified tutors in a seamless and secure manner.

**3\. Problem Statement (WHY This Platform Is Needed)**

**Existing Challenges:**

*   Difficulty for students and parents to **find verified and suitable tutors**
*   Lack of transparency in tutor qualifications, pricing, and reviews
*   Manual coordination for scheduling and payments
*   Limited visibility and growth opportunities for independent tutors
*   Absence of a trusted platform ensuring **safe transactions and accountability**

**Business Problem:**

There is no single, reliable platform that **efficiently connects demand (students/parents)** with **supply (qualified tutors)** while ensuring trust, convenience, and scalability.

**4\. Proposed Solution**

The Tutoring Marketplace Platform will act as a **digital bridge** between tutors and learners by offering:

*   A secure, searchable tutor discovery experience
*   Easy booking and scheduling of tutoring sessions
*   Integrated and trusted payment handling
*   Transparent reviews and ratings
*   Centralized administration and quality control

**5\. Business Objectives**

*   Create a **trusted online marketplace** for tutoring services
*   Simplify the **booking and payment experience** for students and parents
*   Enable tutors to **grow their reach and income**
*   Generate sustainable revenue through a **commission-based model**
*   Establish a scalable foundation for future educational services

**6\. Target Users (WHO the Platform Serves)**

**Primary Users**

1.  **Students**

*   School, college, and university students seeking academic support

1.  **Parents**

*   Parents looking for reliable tutors for their children

1.  **Tutors**

*   Qualified professionals offering online or in-person tutoring

**Secondary Users**

1.  **Platform Admin / Business Owner**

*   Manages tutors, bookings, payments, and platform operations

**7\. How the Platform Helps Each Stakeholder**

**Students & Parents**

*   Easy access to verified tutors across multiple subjects
*   Transparent pricing, reviews, and qualifications
*   Secure and hassle-free booking and payment experience
*   Flexibility to choose online or in-person sessions

**Tutors**

*   A professional platform to showcase skills and qualifications
*   Increased visibility and consistent booking opportunities
*   Automated scheduling and payment tracking
*   Reduced dependency on offline referrals

**Business Owner**

*   Centralized control over operations and quality
*   Clear revenue generation through commissions
*   Data-driven insights via reports and analytics
*   Scalable business model with future expansion potential

**8\. Value Proposition & Business Impact (HOW It Makes a Difference)**

*   **Improves Access to Education**  
    Makes quality tutoring more accessible to students regardless of location.
*   **Builds Trust in the Tutoring Ecosystem**  
    Verified tutors, reviews, and secure payments create confidence for users.
*   **Empowers Independent Tutors**  
    Provides a growth platform without heavy marketing or administrative burden.
*   **Drives Digital Transformation in Education**  
    Replaces fragmented, manual processes with a streamlined digital solution.
*   **Creates a Sustainable Business Model**  
    Commission-based revenue ensures long-term viability and growth.

**9\. High-Level Success Metrics**

*   Number of active tutors and students
*   Booking conversion rate
*   User retention and repeat bookings
*   Platform revenue through commissions
*   Average tutor rating and user satisfaction

**10\. Platform Policies (High-Level)**

The platform shall operate under clearly defined policies to ensure fair treatment of all parties and reduce operational disputes.

**10.1 Cancellation & Refund Policy**

The platform shall enforce a transparent cancellation and refund policy that:
*   Defines clear timelines for penalty-free cancellations
*   Specifies refund amounts based on cancellation timing
*   Establishes fair treatment for both learner-initiated and tutor-initiated cancellations
*   Addresses no-show scenarios for both parties
*   Integrates with the escrow system to automate refund processing

**10.2 Tutor Earnings & Withdrawal**

The platform shall provide tutors with:
*   A transparent earnings tracking system (wallet)
*   Clear rules for when earnings become withdrawable
*   Defined payout schedules and minimum thresholds
*   Compliance with UK financial regulations for payouts

**11\. Legal & Compliance Framework**

As a UK-based platform handling personal data and financial transactions, the business shall ensure:

*   **Data Protection:** Compliance with UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018
*   **User Rights:** Support for data access, correction, deletion, and portability requests
*   **Minors Protection:** Age-appropriate safeguards and parental consent mechanisms
*   **Tutor Classification:** Clear independent contractor relationship with tutors (not employment)
*   **Financial Compliance:** Adherence to UK payment regulations and anti-money laundering requirements

These compliance requirements shall be detailed in the Functional Requirements Document and implemented consistently across all platform features.

**12\. Conclusion**

The Tutoring Marketplace Platform addresses a real and growing need by transforming how students, parents, and tutors connect. By offering a secure, transparent, and user-friendly solution, the platform not only simplifies tutoring services but also **creates meaningful educational impact**, empowers tutors, and establishes a scalable, future-ready business.
